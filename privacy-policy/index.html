Privacy Policy

Welcome to Dr. Mohammad Al Dossary’s website (https://drmdossary.com/). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Any other information you provide when booking consultations or submitting inquiries.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Pages visited on our website
  • Date and time of access

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Respond to inquiries and requests.
  • Schedule and manage appointments or consultations.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Send important notifications, updates, or promotional offers (if consent is provided).

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and will never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party providers to help us deliver services (e.g., hosting or appointment scheduling).
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, court orders, or to protect our legal rights.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience and analyze website performance. Cookies may collect data such as:

  • Pages you visit
  • Time spent on our site
  • Your preferences

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We employ strict security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure, and we cannot ensure complete protection.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. Please review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements).
  • Withdraw your consent for marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@drmdossary.com
