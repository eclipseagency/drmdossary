Corneal Surgeries

Corneal surgeries address a wide range of conditions, including keratoconus, using advanced techniques such as corneal transplantation (full and partial) and UV reinforcement of the cornea. Precise techniques such as laser and femtosecond technology and cross-linking techniques are employed to treat keratoconus, ensuring procedural accuracy and reducing recovery time. This aims to restore clear vision, improve quality of life, and reduce reliance on contact lenses or glasses.
